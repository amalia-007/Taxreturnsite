    📋 How to Lodge Your Tax Return

    1. Create or log in to myGov — my.gov.au →
    2. Link the ATO to your myGov account (Services → Link → ATO).
    3. Open myTax — click Tax → Lodge return → Use myTax.
    4. Check pre-filled data — your employer & bank data is loaded automatically.
    5. Add income & deductions not pre-filled (rental, ABN, gifts).
    6. Submit your return — review summary, then lodge.
    7. Track your refund via the ATO app or myGov (usually 2 weeks).
