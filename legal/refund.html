Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2025

Our commitment

We want you to be satisfied with GET MY TAX. If the Service doesn't meet your needs, we offer a straightforward refund process.

14-day refund guarantee

You are entitled to a full refund of your $19 AUD payment if:

  • You request the refund within 14 days of your purchase date, and
  • You have not yet generated a tax estimate using the calculator.

Once you have used the calculator to produce a tax estimate, the service has been delivered and the purchase is final.

Australian Consumer Law

Our refund policy is in addition to your rights under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL). If the Service has a major failure, you are entitled to a remedy under the ACL regardless of the above conditions. Nothing in this policy limits your statutory rights.

How to request a refund

Email refunds@getmytax.com.au with:

  • The email address associated with your account.
  • Your purchase date or Stripe receipt number (if available).
  • A brief reason for your request (optional but helpful).

We will process eligible refunds within 5 business days. Refunds are returned to your original payment method via Stripe.
