    Leaving Australia? Claim your Super back!

    As a Working Holiday Maker (visa 417 or 462), you can reclaim your superannuation when you leave Australia permanently via the DASP — Departing Australia Superannuation Payment.

    1. 1

      Find your super fund name

      It's on your payslips above. You can also check via myGov → ATO → Super → "Find my super".

    2. 2

      Leave Australia — visa expired or cancelled

      You cannot claim DASP while still in Australia or while your visa is still valid.

    3. 3

      Apply online via the ATO DASP portal

      Submit your claim directly to the ATO — no intermediary needed.

      Open ATO DASP portal →

    ⚠️ Tax on DASP withdrawals: A withholding tax of 65% applies on WHM super withdrawals — but it's still YOUR money! Even after tax, claiming your super back is worth it.
