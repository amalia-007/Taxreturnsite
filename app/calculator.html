    How to Lodge Your Tax Return

    7 steps to lodge via ATO myTax — takes about 20 minutes.

    2024-25
    1. 1

      Create or log in to myGov

      myGov →

      Go to my.gov.au and sign in or create an account.

    2. 2

      Link to ATO

      myGov →

      In myGov, link the ATO service to your account if you haven't already.

    3. 3

      Open myTax

      myTax →

      Select Tax → Lodge return and open myTax for the 2024–25 year.

    4. 4

      Check pre-filled data

      The ATO pre-fills income from employers and banks — review and correct any figures if needed.

    5. 5

      Add deductions

      Enter work-related expenses, charitable donations, and any other eligible deductions. Keep receipts for 5 years.

    6. 6

      Review and submit

      Check your summary, then click Submit. Most refunds arrive within 2 weeks.

    7. 7

      Lodge before 31 October ⚠️

      ATO →

      Late lodgement may result in penalties of $313 per 28 days overdue.
